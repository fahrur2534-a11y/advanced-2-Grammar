ADVANCED GRAMMAR TASK

Fill in the Blank - Connectors
Instruction:
Choose the correct connector from the options given in brackets.

1. The experiment was successful, ______ the results were not fully expected.

2. Many students use AI tools, ______ they must use them responsibly.

3. The number of plastic waste is increasing; ______, environmental pollution is getting worse.

4. She studied very hard, ______ she passed the exam with high scores.

5. Online learning is flexible, ______ it also requires strong self-discipline.

6. The weather was very bad, ______ the school was closed.

7. ______ many students like social media, it can affect their focus on studies.

8. He did not study for the test; ______, he failed it.

9. The teacher explained the lesson clearly, ______ some students still did not understand.

10. ______ technology is developing rapidly, education is also changing.

11. The project was difficult; ______, the students finished it on time.

12. She forgot her homework, ______ she had to do it again at school.

13. Students should read more books, ______ reading improves vocabulary.

14. The internet is useful, ______ it can also be distracting.

15. He did not attend the meeting ______ he was sick.

Answer Key

  1. A
  2. B
  3. A
  4. B
  5. B
  6. A
  7. A
  8. B
  9. A
  10. C
  11. A
  12. A
  13. A
  14. B
  15. A
